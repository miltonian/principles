# Residential Lease Agreement (SAMPLE — for demonstration)

This sample lease is authored for the Principles examples gallery. It deliberately contains tenant-hostile terms so the lease-reviewer evaluator's output can be checked against known ground truth. The planted clauses are §3(b), §5, §7, §9(a), §11, §12, and §14.

---

**RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT** between Cedar Hollow Properties LLC ("Landlord") and the undersigned ("Tenant") for the premises at 412 Marlowe Street, Unit 2B.

**§1. Term.** Twelve (12) months commencing October 1.

**§2. Rent.** $2,150 per month, due on the 1st; a late fee of $75 applies after the 3rd.

**§3. Renewal.**
(a) This lease renews automatically for successive twelve-month terms.
(b) Tenant may decline renewal only by certified written notice delivered no earlier than 120 and no later than 90 days before term end; notice outside this window is void and the lease renews at a rent of Landlord's sole determination.

**§4. Utilities.** Tenant pays electricity, gas, and internet. Landlord pays water and trash.

**§5. Repairs and Maintenance.** Tenant shall bear the full cost of all repairs and maintenance to the premises and its systems (including plumbing, electrical, heating, and appliances) regardless of cause, ordinary wear and tear included, except structural roof failure.

**§6. Quiet Enjoyment.** Tenant is entitled to quiet enjoyment of the premises subject to the terms herein.

**§7. Entry.** Landlord or its agents may enter the premises at any time without notice for inspection, maintenance, showings, or any business purpose.

**§8. Pets.** No pets without written consent; approved pets require a $500 nonrefundable fee.

**§9. Security Deposit.**
(a) Deposit of $4,300 is forfeited in full if Tenant vacates before the end of any renewal term for any reason, in addition to any damages owed.
(b) Deposit returnable within 60 days of move-out otherwise.

**§10. Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain renter's insurance of at least $100,000 liability.

**§11. Rent Adjustment.** Landlord may increase rent at any time during the term upon 15 days' notice to reflect market conditions, taxes, or operating costs, at Landlord's sole discretion.

**§12. Dispute Resolution.** Tenant waives the right to a jury trial and to participate in any class or collective action against Landlord; all disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration before an arbitrator selected and paid for... by Landlord.

**§13. Alterations.** No alterations without written consent.

**§14. Attorney's Fees.** In any dispute, Tenant shall pay Landlord's attorney's fees and costs regardless of outcome.

**§15. Governing Law.** The laws of the state of the premises govern this lease.

*Signatures omitted for the sample.*
